Case Studies

As I work through projects that have a full project scope I like to keep a journal of the process. It helps me reflect so I can learn and improve as well as give me a gauge on velocity all while allowing me to tell a story and explain the reasons why certain decisions were made. You never work in isolation. In every project being able to work with people and navigation challenges is as important as the actual results themselves. Not every project ends up the way you desire it to, or the way it was planned. I hope these case studies help explain how I work.
