Teacher Enhanced Math Assessments

Discovery Education allows teachers to create custom assesments for the students. Until this project the only part of STEM that was not covered was Mathematics. I was part of the team that changed that.

Due to my confidentiality agreement and the sensitive nature of my work, I have omitted and modified information.
These designs are a reinterpretation of the original and are populated with stub data.

Overview

Discovery Education offers countless products for schools and districts but one of the most used products, Assessments, was also one that was in dire need of additional content and an overall update to the experience. Assessments allow teachers to construct a custom assessment from any of the STEM standards their particular school district is part of. Mathematics questions and curriculum were always available to teachers, but not in the form of an organized assessment which meant that while teachers could use the material, they could not gather, save or record anything. Math had been requested for some time and we were finally able to provide this service.
